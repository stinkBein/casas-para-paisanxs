About Me

This is me working in a soup kitchen for migrants in Nogales, Sonora, Mexico. I have worked for more than 15 years providing humanitarian and legal assistance to (im)migrants in the USA and Mexico. Currently, I work as a legal assistant for Merced Law PLLC, I manage the Centro de Información para Migrantes, a free service that answers peoples questions about migration processes in Mexico and the USA, and – now – I am a real estate agent. I am witness to the infinite number of abuses committed against people just because they are (im)migrants and it is my commitment to counteract injustices and inequality by making sure that you have access to the information you need to have more stability and help you obtain the best deals when buying and selling your homes.
